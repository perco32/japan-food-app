Ingredients (for 2 servings)

  • Warm cooked rice: 2 large bowls (400g / approx. 14 oz)
  • Thinly sliced beef: 200g (approx. 7 oz)
  • Onion: 1/2
  • Red pickled ginger (Beni shoga): to taste

☆ Seasonings

  • Sake: 1 tbsp
  • Mirin: 2 tbsp
  • Sugar: 2 tsp
  • Soy sauce: 2 tbsp
  • Water: 200cc (approx. 0.85 US cups)
  • Grated ginger: 1/2 tsp

Instructions

  1. Cut the onion into wedges.
  2. In a pot, combine the onion and all ☆ seasonings. Bring to a boil, then cover and cook over medium heat for 5 minutes.
  3. Add the beef, breaking it apart. Skim off any scum and cook over low heat for 10 minutes.
  4. Serve the warm rice in bowls, top with the mixture from step 3, and garnish with red pickled ginger as desired.
