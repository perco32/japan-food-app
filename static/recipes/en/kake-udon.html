Ingredients (for 1 serving)

  • Frozen udon noodle block: 1
  • Mentsuyu (noodle soup base, 3x concentrate): 100ml (approx. 0.42 US cups)
  • Water: 200ml (approx. 0.85 US cups)
  • Optional toppings: Chopped green onions, kamaboko (fish cake), etc.

About Mentsuyu Concentrate Types

Adjust the water amount according to the concentration of your mentsuyu. For a hot broth (approx. 300ml total), a general guide is:
・**For 2x concentrate:** Mentsuyu 150ml, Water 150ml (1:1 ratio)
・**For 3x concentrate:** Mentsuyu 100ml, Water 200ml (1:2 ratio)
・**For 4x concentrate:** Mentsuyu 75ml, Water 225ml (1:3 ratio)

Instructions

  1. Cook the frozen udon according to the package instructions (either in a microwave or by boiling) and place it in a bowl.
  2. In a small pot, combine the mentsuyu and water. Bring to a boil.
  3. Pour the hot broth from step 2 over the udon in the bowl.
  4. Garnish with your favorite toppings like chopped green onions and serve immediately.

Arrangement & Topping Ideas

Kake Udon can be enjoyed with a wide variety of toppings. Feel free to try these!

  • Wakame: Rehydrate dried wakame seaweed in water and add it on top.
  • Tenkasu (Tempura Scraps): Adds a nice crunch.
  • Spinach: Add some quickly boiled spinach.
  • Sesame Seeds: Sprinkle some toasted sesame seeds for aroma.
  • Tsukimi Udon: Drop a raw egg yolk in the center to make "moon-viewing" udon.
  • Pork Belly & Black Pepper: Top with boiled pork belly slices and a generous amount of freshly ground black pepper.
