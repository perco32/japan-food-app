Ingredients (for 1 serving)

  • Frozen udon noodle block: 1
  • Mentsuyu (noodle soup base, 3x concentrate): 50ml (approx. 3.4 tbsp)
  • Cold water: 100ml (approx. 0.42 US cups)

Favorite Condiments (Yakumi)

  • Chopped green onions
  • Grated ginger
  • Toasted sesame seeds
  • Shredded nori seaweed

Instructions

  1. Boil the Udon: Bring a large pot of water to a boil. Cook the frozen udon according to the package instructions.
  2. Chill the Udon: Drain the cooked udon in a colander. Rinse under cold running water to remove excess starch. Finally, plunge the noodles into a bowl of ice water for a few seconds to make them perfectly chewy, then drain well.

    Tip: This step is the most important for achieving the firm, chewy texture that is key to delicious zaru udon.

  3. Prepare the Dipping Sauce: In a small cup, mix the mentsuyu and cold water.
  4. Serve: Place the chilled udon on a zaru (bamboo tray) or a plate. Serve with the dipping sauce and your favorite condiments on the side.
