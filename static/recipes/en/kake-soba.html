Ingredients (for 1 serving)

  • Dried soba noodles: 100g (approx. 3.5 oz)
  • Mentsuyu (noodle soup base, 3x concentrate): 100ml (approx. 0.42 US cups)
  • Water: 300ml (approx. 1.25 US cups)
  • Optional toppings: Chopped green onions, etc.

Instructions

  1. Bring a large pot of water to a boil. Cook the soba noodles according to package directions.
  2. Drain the cooked soba in a colander and rinse under cold running water to remove excess starch.
  3. In a separate small pot, combine the mentsuyu and water. Bring to a boil.
  4. Place the rinsed soba noodles in a bowl (you can re-heat them quickly by pouring hot water over them if desired) and pour the hot broth over them. Garnish with toppings and serve immediately.

Arrangement & Topping Ideas

Kake Soba serves as a base for many variations.

  • Tanuki Soba: Add a generous amount of tenkasu (crunchy tempura bits).
  • Tempura Soba: Add your favorite tempura, especially shrimp tempura.
  • Tsukimi Soba: Drop a raw egg yolk in the center to make "moon-viewing" soba.
  • Wakame Soba: Add rehydrated wakame seaweed.
